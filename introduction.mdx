Echo is a proactive AI assistant that connects to your favorite tools, learns from your behavior, and executes multi-step tasks automatically—always with your permission.

## What makes Echo different?

Most chatbots are reactive. You ask, they respond. Echo is different. Think of it as an ambient, proactive virtual assistant that:

- **Listens continuously** to activity in your connected apps
- **Proposes actions** based on real-time events and context
- **Executes automatically** (with your approval) across multiple tools
- **Learns from you** using Thoughts—notes, links, and memories you add

<Note>
Echo uses **Actions** for automated task execution and **Thoughts** for long-term context and personalization.
</Note>

## Example workflow

Here's how Echo works in practice:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your apps">
    During onboarding, connect everyday apps like Gmail, Google Calendar, Google Drive, and Todoist. You have full control to customize, modify, or revoke access anytime.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Trigger fires">
    When activity occurs in a connected app (e.g., a new calendar event), Echo receives a trigger notification.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Echo analyzes context">
    Echo gathers relevant context from your connected apps and your saved Thoughts to understand what action makes sense.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Action proposal">
    Echo proposes a multi-step action, such as checking availability, RSVPing to the event, and preparing a meeting deck using past documents.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="You approve or modify">
    You receive a notification with the proposed action. Approve it, reject it, or provide additional context to refine it.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Echo executes">
    Once approved, Echo executes the action across multiple tools automatically and reports back with the results.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
You can configure Echo to **ask first** (propose actions and wait for approval) or **auto-execute** (run actions immediately based on your preferences).
</Info>

## Tech stack

Echo is built with modern technologies for performance and scalability:

- **Frontend**: React Native with Expo, TypeScript, NativeWind (Tailwind CSS)
- **State Management**: Zustand (local UI + auth), TanStack Query (data fetching)
- **Backend**: Node.js, Express.js, TypeScript, Drizzle ORM
- **Database**: PostgreSQL via Supabase
- **Authentication**: Supabase Auth
- **Integrations**: Composio (OAuth + tool execution), Supermemory (memory management)
- **AI Models**: Support for OpenAI, Google Gemini, and Anthropic Claude

## Key features

### Proactive automation
Echo doesn't wait for commands. It monitors your connected apps and proactively suggests actions based on real-time events.

### Cross-app workflows
Execute multi-step tasks that span multiple applications. Echo handles OAuth, API calls, and error handling automatically.

### Personalized context
Thoughts continuously augment Echo's understanding, guiding and refining actions over time based on your preferences and history.

### Fine-grained permissions
Connect apps with precise OAuth scopes. Modify or revoke permissions anytime from the Toolkits screen.

### Multi-turn conversations
Refine actions through natural conversation. Echo maintains context across multiple turns for complex workflows.

<Tip>
Start by connecting 2-3 apps you use daily, then add a few Thoughts about your preferences. Echo will learn your workflow patterns quickly.
</Tip>
