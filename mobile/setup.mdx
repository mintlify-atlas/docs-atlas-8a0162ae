The Echo mobile app brings your proactive AI assistant to your phone, enabling you to manage actions, save thoughts, and stay connected wherever you go.

## Download the App

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose Your Platform">
    Echo is available for both iOS and Android devices:
    - **iOS**: Download from the Apple App Store
    - **Android**: Download from the Google Play Store
  </Step>

  <Step title="Install the App">
    Tap the download button and wait for the installation to complete. The app icon will appear on your home screen once installed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Launch Echo">
    Open the app to begin the setup process. You'll be greeted with introductory screens explaining Echo's core features.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## System Requirements

### iOS
- iOS 13.0 or later
- Compatible with iPhone and iPad
- Supports both portrait and landscape orientations on iPad

### Android
- Android 5.0 (Lollipop) or later
- Minimum 2GB RAM recommended
- Portrait orientation

## First Launch Experience

When you first open Echo, you'll see three introductory screens:

1. **Meet Echo**: Learn about Echo as your proactive virtual assistant that works in the background
2. **Actions**: Understand how Echo executes multi-step tasks using your connected tools
3. **Thoughts**: Discover how to save links, notes, and context that Echo remembers forever

<Info>
These intro screens appear only once. After viewing them, you'll proceed directly to sign-in.
</Info>

## Signing In

After the introductory screens, you'll need to create an account or sign in:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Email Sign-In">
    Enter your email address to receive a magic link for authentication. No password required.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check Your Email">
    Open the email from Echo and tap the verification link. This will authenticate your session.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Return to App">
    The app will automatically detect your successful sign-in and proceed to onboarding.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Echo uses passwordless authentication for enhanced security and convenience. Keep your email secure as it's your primary access method.
</Note>

## App Permissions

During setup, Echo may request certain permissions:

### Notifications (Recommended)
Allow notifications to receive real-time updates about:
- Pending actions requiring approval
- Completed tasks
- Important updates from connected tools

<Tip>
You can configure notification preferences during onboarding or later in Settings.
</Tip>

### Share Extension (Optional)
On iOS, Echo can integrate with the share sheet, allowing you to:
- Share links from Safari, Chrome, or other browsers
- Save content directly to Echo from any app
- Add context with notes while sharing

## Appearance Settings

Echo supports both light and dark themes:
- **System** (default): Automatically matches your device settings
- **Light**: Always use light mode
- **Dark**: Always use dark mode

Theme settings can be changed anytime in the Settings screen.

## Troubleshooting

### App Won't Open
- Ensure your device meets the minimum system requirements
- Try restarting your device
- Reinstall the app if issues persist

### Sign-In Issues
- Check your email spam/junk folder for the magic link
- Ensure you're using the same device to open the link
- Request a new magic link if the previous one expired

### Performance Issues
- Close and restart the app
- Clear app cache in device settings
- Ensure you have a stable internet connection

<Warning>
Echo requires an active internet connection to sync actions and thoughts. Offline functionality is limited.
</Warning>
