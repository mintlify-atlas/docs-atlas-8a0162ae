The Settings screen provides full control over your Echo experience, from visual appearance to automation behavior and notification preferences.

## Accessing Settings

Navigate to Settings:
1. Tap the Settings tab in the bottom navigation bar
2. The Settings icon is typically on the far right
3. All configuration options are organized by category

## Profile Information

Your profile appears at the top of the Settings screen:

### Profile Display
- **Avatar**: Circular icon with user initials
- **Email**: Your registered account email
- **Account Status**: Displays if verified/active

<Note>
Your email cannot be changed from the app. Contact support if you need to update your account email.
</Note>

## Appearance Settings

Control how Echo looks on your device:

### Theme Selection

Choose your preferred color scheme:

**Light Mode**
- Bright, high-contrast interface
- White backgrounds with dark text
- Best for daytime use
- Easier on eyes in bright environments

**Dark Mode**
- Dark backgrounds with light text
- Reduced eye strain in low light
- Battery savings on OLED screens
- Modern, sleek appearance

<Tip>
Echo's theme automatically adapts if you keep it set to "System" mode, matching your device's appearance settings.
</Tip>

### Changing Theme

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Navigate to the Settings tab.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Locate Appearance Section">
    The theme toggles appear at the top under "Appearance."
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Theme">
    Tap Light or Dark to immediately apply the theme.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The theme change is instant—no app restart required.

## Preferences

Customize how Echo understands and assists you:

### Persona

Update your work role or context:
- Developer
- Founder / Manager
- Designer / Creative
- Student / Research
- Sales / Ops
- Personal

**How to Change:**
1. Tap "Persona" row in Preferences
2. Modal appears with all options
3. Select your new persona
4. Change saves immediately
5. Echo updates toolkit recommendations

<Info>
Changing persona affects which actions Echo prioritizes and which toolkits are recommended.
</Info>

### Work Location

Specify where you spend most of your time:
- Inbox (email-focused)
- Calendar (meeting-driven)
- Docs / Sheets (document-centric)
- Tasks (task management)
- Code (development environments)

**How to Change:**
1. Tap "Work Location" in Preferences
2. Choose from available options
3. Selection saves automatically

### Automation

Control how Echo executes actions:

**Ask First (Recommended)**
- Echo proposes actions and waits for approval
- You review every action before execution
- Maximum control and visibility
- Best for new users

**Auto-Execute**
- Approved action types run automatically
- Reduces manual intervention
- Faster task completion
- Requires trust in Echo's judgment

<Warning>
Only enable auto-execute after you're comfortable with Echo's action patterns. Start with "Ask First" to build confidence.
</Warning>

**How to Change:**
1. Tap "Automation" in Preferences
2. Select "Ask first" or "Auto-execute"
3. Confirm your selection

### Notifications

Configure when and how Echo notifies you:

**All Notifications**
- Every action triggers a notification
- Real-time awareness of all activity
- Can be overwhelming for active users

**Urgent Only**
- Only time-sensitive or high-priority actions
- Balanced notification volume
- Recommended for most users

**Daily Digest**
- Single summary notification per day
- Review all actions in one batch
- Minimal interruption

**Silent**
- No push notifications
- Check Echo manually at your convenience
- For users who prefer pull-based updates

**How to Change:**
1. Tap "Notifications" in Preferences
2. Choose your notification frequency
3. Preference saves immediately

<Tip>
Your notification preference is separate from system notification permissions. Both must be enabled to receive alerts.
</Tip>

## Push Notifications

Manage system-level notification permissions:

### Enable Push Notifications

If you initially declined notification permissions:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tap Enable Push Notifications">
    In the "Push Notifications" section of Settings.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Grant Permission">
    - **iOS**: System dialog appears, tap "Allow"
    - **Android**: System prompt appears, tap "Allow"
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Registration">
    Success message indicates notifications are enabled.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### If Notifications Aren't Working

**iOS:**
1. Open iOS Settings app
2. Scroll to Echo
3. Tap Notifications
4. Enable "Allow Notifications"
5. Configure alert style, sounds, badges

**Android:**
1. Open Android Settings
2. Navigate to Apps > Echo
3. Tap Notifications
4. Enable all notification categories

<Note>
Even with "Silent" notification preference, you should enable system notifications for urgent alerts.
</Note>

## Developer Options

Advanced features for testing and troubleshooting:

### Push Notifications Test

Verify your notification setup:
1. Tap "Push Notifications Test" in Developer section
2. Screen appears with test options
3. Send test notifications
4. Verify they appear on your device

<Info>
Developer options are visible to all users for transparency and troubleshooting.
</Info>

## Account Management

### Sign Out

Log out of your Echo account:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tap Sign Out">
    In the Account section at the bottom of Settings.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Action">
    Alert dialog asks for confirmation.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign Out Completes">
    - Local session cleared
    - Returned to sign-in screen
    - Thoughts and actions remain on server
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Signing out clears your local session but doesn't delete your account or data. Sign back in anytime to restore access.
</Warning>

### Account Deletion

To permanently delete your Echo account:
1. Contact support at support@echo.app
2. Request account deletion
3. Confirm via email
4. Account and all data are permanently removed

<Note>
Account deletion cannot be undone. Export any important data before requesting deletion.
</Note>

## Best Practices

### Regular Review
- Revisit preferences monthly
- Adjust as your workflow evolves
- Test new automation modes cautiously

### Notification Optimization
- Start with "Urgent Only"
- Adjust based on action volume
- Enable system notifications even if using "Silent"

### Theme Comfort
- Use dark mode in low-light environments
- Light mode in bright settings
- System mode for automatic switching

### Security
- Sign out on shared devices
- Don't share account credentials
- Review toolkit connections periodically

## Troubleshooting

### Settings Not Saving
- Check internet connection
- Try closing and reopening Settings
- Sign out and back in if persistent

### Notifications Not Received
- Verify system permissions are granted
- Check notification preference isn't set to "Silent"
- Run notification test in Developer section
- Ensure Do Not Disturb is disabled on device

### Theme Not Applying
- Restart the app
- Try toggling between themes
- Clear app cache in device settings

### Can't Change Preferences
- Ensure you're signed in
- Check for app updates
- Contact support if issue persists

## Privacy and Data

### What's Stored
- User preferences and settings
- Toolkit authorization tokens
- Thoughts and action history

### What's Not Stored
- Passwords (uses magic link authentication)
- Full content from connected tools
- Browsing history outside shared items

### Data Control
- Export your data anytime (contact support)
- Delete specific thoughts or actions
- Disconnect toolkits to revoke access
- Request full account deletion

<Info>
Echo follows industry-standard security practices. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest.
</Info>
